ABOUT ME

I hope this message finds you in good health. I am Zeeshan Haider. I hail from Lahore and graduated as a Mechanical Engineer from UET Lahore. Subsequently, I began my career as a research engineer at Cerad (Center for Energy Research and Development). In this role, I developed various CAD assemblies and oversaw their manufacturing processes. Later, I transitioned to the industrial sector, serving as an assistant development manager in an automotive company is a pivotal role within the organization.

Throughout my four years at the university, I actively engaged in weekend projects. Our team participated in the SDC organized by GIKI, winning at the Pakistan level and qualifying for the final round in Houston, Texas in 2015. The subsequent year, our team excelled once again, reaching the final round in Phoenix, Arizona.
